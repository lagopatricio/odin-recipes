Cookies Recipe

This simple, tasty recipe yields 12 to 16 cookies, but is so simple that it can be doubled (or tripled!) easily.

Ingridients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. sPreheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Beat peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.

    Stir egg, vanilla extract, and baking soda into peanut butter mixture; stir in chocolate chips.

  3. Drop mixture by small rounded spoonfuls onto a baking sheet about 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are flattened and golden, about 8 minutes.

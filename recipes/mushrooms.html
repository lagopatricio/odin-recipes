Mushrooms Recipe

This recipe for a buttery, delicious, mushroom side dish couldn't be any simpler. It goes well with fish fillets, baked chicken, steak, and just about any other main dish.

Ingridients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt

Steps

  1. In a skillet, over medium heat, melt the butter.

    Add mushrooms, and sprinkle with seasoned salt.

    Cook, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms soften and become darker in color, about 10 minutes.
